Oppo A3s Specifications in Pakistan

Find the features of Oppo A3s, detailed information about the specs and hardware features is given below.

Operating System

Oppo A3s operates on Android V8.1 Oreo, and has Octa-core 1.8 Ghz Octa Core Cortex-A53 CPU, which gives excellent speed and up to date security to the mobile device.

It has latest Qualcomm SDM450 Snapdragon 450 Chipset, and Adreno 506 GPU for great performance.

Design, Display & Body

Oppo A3s has 6.2 Inches IPS LCD Capacitive Touchscreen, 16M Colors, Multitouch screen, which gives excellent result to its users. It has 720 x 1520 Pixels (~271 PPI) resolution, along with protection. Which gives best quality display result and protection to the phone display. The other display features include .

The Dimensions of A3s are 156.2 x 75.6 x 8.2 mm, mobile phones are getting light day by day, the era of weightless phones has arrived as the technology is improving day by day The weight of this phone is just 168 Gram. The available colors of A3s are Red, Dark Purple which are elegant, practical and funky as well.

Camera

Camera is always an important factor before you choose a new phone, Oppo A3s has great camera result, and provides excellent photos & portraits from front camera and back camera. So If you love taking Selfie with your mobile than here is a good news, A3s Selfie camera is 8 MP, f/2.2, which provides perfect pictures and great result during Skype, WhatsApp, Facebook, Viber etc video calls.

The main camera of A3s is a plus point. The phone has Dual 13 MP (f/2.2, AF) + 2 MP (f/2.4, no AF, depth sensor), LED Flash. Means you can get great shots with this Dual 13 camera, even in low light you can use the LED flash to get excellent photos. The other camera features include Geo-tagging, touch focus, face detection, HDR, panorama, Video (1080p@30fps).

Battery

Long battery and Fast charging are important features of every mobile phone these days, Oppo A3s has a huge (Li-ion Non removable), 4230 mAh, which gives you enough freedom on single charge.

SIM Card & GSM Network

Oppo A3s has Dual Sim, Dual Standby (Nano-SIM). A3s has 2G, 3G and 4G LTE network support, which gives you freedom to use on any mobile operator in Pakistan, including Mobilink Jazz, Ufone, Telenor, Zong and Warid. There is no coverage issue with this phone anywhere in Pakistan.

Connectivity and Wireless Network Features

Everything has become wireless these days, from wireless charging to wireless internet. Oppo A3s has perfect wireless functions for its users. Wireless Internet WiFi or Wlan features include Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, WiFi Direct, hotspot. Means you can use any of the above wifi band on this device, also use it as hotspot to connect more wireless devices to share the internet.

Bluetooth is important to share files, photos, music and videos etc. A3s bluetooth features are latest Bluetooth v4.2 with A2DP, LE. Which provides fastest file transfer and excellent result on wireless handsfree and headset to hear music.

GPS is need of everyone these days for tracking of location and to use Google Maps. A3s has Latest + A-GPS support, which provides 100% accuracy in location tracking, navigation, maps and car assistance.

You can connect this phone with your computer via USB, the USB features microUSB 2.0, USB On-The-Go.

Internal Memory and External Storage

Oppo A3s comes with 16GB Built-in, 2GB RAM, which gives you freedom to save your pictures, photos, videos, mp3 songs and other important data. You can store the data inside the phone memory or on the cloud as per your requirement. This device also has external storage, which is microSD Card, (supports up to 256GB). So if you are out of internal space you can always use the external storage to save your important data.

Other Specifications & Features

Oppo A3s has many hidden features, which are important for security, safety, entertainment and gaming. The sensors play a vital role in every mobile’s specs, A3s has following sensors Accelerometer, Compass, Proximity.

Everyone loves Mobile games, gaming industry is expanding day by day. A3s gives best internal games, and downloading game option to its users, so they can entertain themself when they are free or getting bored.

Following are the other features which are available in Oppo A3s Active noise cancellation with dedicated mic, Document viewer, Photo viewer/editor.
